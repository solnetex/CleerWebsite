About Cleer

The AI assistant that sees, understands, and acts

Revolutionary AI Technology

Cleer represents a breakthrough in artificial intelligence, combining advanced computer vision with contextual understanding to create an AI assistant that truly sees what you see. Unlike traditional AI tools that require explicit commands, Cleer operates with minimal input, understanding your workflow and providing assistance automatically.

Visual Intelligence at Its Core

Our proprietary visual processing technology enables Cleer to interpret screen content, documents, and applications in real-time. This visual understanding allows Cleer to provide contextually relevant assistance without disrupting your workflow.

Personalized Learning

Cleer learns your writing style, work patterns, and preferences to provide increasingly personalized assistance. The more you interact with Cleer, the better it becomes at anticipating your needs and providing proactive support.
